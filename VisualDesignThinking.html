ID 2401: Visual Design Thinking

Robot Design Project


To begin our assignment, I had to sketch out 10 different assistive robot ideas. Next, I had to choose an idea to continue with and I had to come up with different modifications to that idea. I chose to create a robot that is able to paint your nails with different colors and designs while you relax. After finalizing my robot's design, I then created a story board to express how a user would interact with the robot.




Toy Story Board


Our assignment was to choose a toy commercial from the 1980's or 1990's, to identify the rising action, climax, and the falling action of the commercial. Once we chose our commercial, we had to take screenshots of several scenes throughout the commercial and then create a story board depicting the different stages of the commercial. I chose this Game Boy commercial https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lnkk8CtPNQg




100 Thumbnails Project


For this group project, we had to identify a need we wanted to address and create 100 thumbnail sketches of potential solutions to the problem chosen. The problem my group chose to address was the need for a young adult population to be able own vases that allowed for individualized and artistic expression.
